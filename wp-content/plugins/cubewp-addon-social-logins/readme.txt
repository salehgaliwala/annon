CubeWP Social Login addon lets you enhance cubewp Login and Registration.

== Description ==

CubeWP Social Login is a powerful add-on for the CubeWP Framework that allows users to sign in and sign up using their Google and Facebook accounts. It also provides the option to connect existing accounts with these two popular social media platforms. With CubeWP Social Login, you can effortlessly enhance the user experience on your website by simplifying the login process and reducing the need for users to remember additional credentials.

Features:

Seamless Social Login: Enable your users to sign in and sign up with their Google and Facebook accounts seamlessly. This eliminates the need for users to create new accounts or remember additional login details.

Connect Existing Accounts: Users can easily connect their existing website accounts with their Google and Facebook accounts. This feature allows for a hassle-free account management experience.

Enhanced User Experience: CubeWP Social Login enhances the overall user experience by providing a simplified and streamlined login process. It eliminates the need for users to fill out lengthy registration forms and enables quick access to your website's content.

Future Updates: We are committed to continually improving and expanding the features of CubeWP Social Login. In future updates, we plan to introduce additional social login methods such as Twitter and other popular platforms. Stay tuned for these exciting updates!

Installation:

Download the CubeWP Social Login Plugin from the CubeWP website or the WordPress Plugin Directory.

Upload the plugin folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory of your WordPress installation.

Activate the CubeWP Social Login Plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in your WordPress admin panel.

Configure the plugin settings by navigating to 'CubeWP Social Login' in the CubeWP Settings admin menu.

Customize the behavior of the social login buttons to match your website's design and requirements.

Save your changes and enjoy the seamless social login experience on your website!

We hope you enjoy using CubeWP Social Login to simplify the login process for your users and improve their overall experience on your website. If you have any questions, feedback, or suggestions, please reach out to our support team at https://help.cubewp.com.

Note: CubeWP Social Login requires the CubeWP Framework to be installed and activated on your WordPress website for proper functionality.
